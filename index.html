About Tulongeni

At Tulongeni Yo 2030, we specialize in two distinct yet harmonious services. In our farming division, we focus on the essential practices of ploughing and threshing, ensuring the cultivation of quality crops through time-honored methods. On the other hand, our catering service stands independently, offering a diverse range of culinary experiences tailored to your unique needs. While farming and catering may seem worlds apart, we bring them together under the umbrella of our commitment to excellence. Join us as we redefine the boundaries of agriculture and gastronomy, offering unparalleled expertise in both ploughing fields and crafting delightful dining experiences.
